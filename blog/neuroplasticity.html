Neuroplasticity and Growth Mindset

What is Neuroplasticity?

Neuroplasticity is the process in which all permanant learning is taking place in the brain such as learning a language or an instrument. Studies have shown that just by knowing that Neuroplasticity even excists and that intelligence is malleable that people did better in school than those who didnt. I feel like a growth mindset is about understanding that your on a knowledge journey and that although you might not understand something yet you will in the future.
